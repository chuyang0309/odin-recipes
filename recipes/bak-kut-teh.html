Bak Kut Teh

Bak Kut Teh, a honestly-good dish that became a joke after an attempt to list it as one of the Malaysian traditional cuisines.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Bak Kut Teh flavour packet
  • Tofu
  • Vege
  • Mushroom

Steps

  1. Place pork and flavour packet into boiling water and boil for 10mins.
  2. Place vege and mushrooms, boil for 10mins
  3. Place tofu and boil for 5mins
  4. Voila!
