Azuma Seafood Rice

Legendary price to performance ratio seafood rice from one of the pioneer japanese food restaurant in Malaysia.

Ingredients

  • Japanese Rice
  • Salmon
  • Octopus arm
  • Cheese Sauce
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Place white rice in bowl and add sweet sauce to fill base of bowl
  2. Add seafood listed above
  3. Voila!
