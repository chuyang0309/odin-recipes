Nasi Lemak :D

Nasi Lemak, the iconic food of Malaysia. My only love.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Ikan Bilis
  • Sambal
  • Boiled Egg
  • Fried Chicken
  • Peanuts

Steps

  1. Place cooked white rice onto a banana leaf.
  2. Sprinkle ikan bilis and peanuts, add fried chicken, sambal sauce and peanuts.
  3. Voila!
